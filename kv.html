Charaka's Life

Charaka was an ancient Indian physician who is known for his contributions to Ayurveda, particularly the Charaka Samhita, a foundational text.

Key Teachings

Charaka emphasized the balance of doshas, the importance of digestion, and the holistic approach to health.

Dosha Concept

Charaka identified three doshas (Vata, Pitta, Kapha) as key factors affecting health.

Timeline of Charaka's Life

Birth of Charaka

Charaka is believed to have been born around 100 BCE.

Charaka Samhita

Charaka compiled the Charaka Samhita, a key text in Ayurvedic medicine.

Legacy

Charaka's work continues to influence modern Ayurvedic practices.
