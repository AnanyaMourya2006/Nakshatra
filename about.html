ABOUT

The Virtual Herbal Garden—also known as the MAPA Garden or Nakshatra Van—is a dynamic and immersive digital platform that bridges the gap between tradition and technology. At its core, it is an interactive experience where users can explore a rich tapestry of herbal flora, including sacred and medicinal plants from various cultural and healing traditions.

Through thoughtfully curated content, visually engaging aids, and scientifically backed data, the platform offers deep insights into the **origin**, **cultivation techniques**, **medicinal properties**, and **holistic applications** of herbs in everyday life. Whether you're a passionate herbalist, a curious learner, or simply someone seeking natural wellness solutions, this space invites you to reconnect with nature’s wisdom.

Our mission is to inspire a renewed appreciation for ancient plant knowledge, while promoting sustainable living and well-being. The Virtual Herbal Garden is not just an educational tool—it’s a journey into the green heart of nature.
